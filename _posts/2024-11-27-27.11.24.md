Been rewatching old movies, looking for a glimmer of my childhood. 
Anhedonic to the ninth degree. I'm not jumping in front of trains but I'm really bored.
I feel like I've done everything there is to do. There are no real challenges. There never really were.

I suppose we're all here to fart around and have fun, which comes from time to time, although
it definitely feels like we're uncomfortable more often than not and the pleasures come
few and far between. Oh, well, I won't be the one to grumble about it. We still have lots of nice,
fancy things to keep us entertained. Lots of shiny things to buy, lots of holidays to enjoy.

Looking forward to Christmas, seeing my family and so on. Also returning to my hometown, finally.
Not much else to report. Stalking Palantir as usual, keeping my eye on SoFi. Bitcoin almost 100K.

Nothing new to ever really write about. What am I even doing this for, to consult back to myself?
Life really is like a big store nobody gets to shop at. It's very pay-to-play, and all the
earnings are paid with through suffering. Very self defeating game, to say the least, but again,
I'm looking to remain optimistic. Christmas will be fun, New Year's will be fun, yada, yada.

But honestly folks, what the hell are we doing here?
